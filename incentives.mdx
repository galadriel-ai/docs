# Earn a stake in ETH for AI

During testnet phase for the first 6 months, Galadriel will allocate 1% of its total token supply to all GPU nodes that join and provide LLM inference to the network.

- Rewards calculated daily
- Over the course of 182 days (6m)
- ~0,0055% a day distributed among all active nodes

## Daily share
**Individual node's daily share is based on:**

1. Uptime of the node
2. Successful inferences made
3. Performance of the node 
    - Throughput
    - Latency

<Tip>
    Tokens will be distributed based on the % accumulated. The exact date for token is not yet public.
</Tip>
